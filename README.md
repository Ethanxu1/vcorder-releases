# VCorder

**Record. Transcribe. Understand.**

VCorder turns your lectures, meetings, and interviews into searchable transcripts and structured notes — so you can focus on the conversation instead of taking notes.

## What it does

### Record from anywhere
Capture your microphone, your computer's audio, or both at the same time. Great for in-person classes, online meetings, and anything in between.

### See the transcript as you go
Watch words appear live while you record. When multiple people are talking, VCorder separates them out so you can tell who said what — and rename them later.

### Smart bookmarks
Drop a bookmark on any important moment. VCorder gives each one a short auto-generated label so you can find it again at a glance.

### Notes that organize themselves
After recording, get a clean set of notes broken down by topic — with definitions, examples, comparison tables, formulas, and timestamps that jump straight back to the audio.

### Action items, surfaced automatically
Every recording gets scanned for to-dos. They all roll up into one shared list so nothing slips through the cracks.

### Bring in your own files
Drop in audio, video, or existing transcripts (`.srt`, `.vtt`) and VCorder will process them like any other recording. Attach slides, PDFs, or images and they become part of your notes — fully searchable.

### Chat with your recordings
Ask questions across one recording or your whole library. VCorder pulls answers straight from your transcripts and materials, with citations back to the source.

### Find anything, fast
Full-text search across every recording, transcript, and note. Filter by tag, folder, or date.

### Stay organized
Folders, tags (lecture / meeting / interview), and starred favorites keep your library tidy as it grows.

### Pick up where you left off
Sign in and your library follows you across devices.
